Candle

3D printed plastic, Acrylic rod, LED diode, rechargable battery. 2022

With a looming energy crisis this winter and the many thoughts of buying fail-safe backup candles, I wanted to explore what a modern digital candle might be like. Safe, portable, rechargable, bright enough to navigate at night.

Press the top of the Acrylic rod to turn on and off

20cm(h) x 6cm(w) x 4cm(d)

Frame

Brass, Oak, 3D printed plastic, LED bulb, fabric cord. 2021

I designed this light with contrast in mind, two materials – wood and brass – and two curves – top and bottom. The half mirror bulb is hung at eye level, to further emphasis the contrast. My favorite detail is the cord, hidden in a channel in the back of one of the legs.

Designed for corner locations

200cm(h) x 45cm(w) x 5cm(d)

Stem

Brass, 3D printed plastic, filament bulb, fabric cord. 2018

A quick experiment with my brother’s metal lathe and my 3D printer. This slimness of the custom-made E27 socket allowed for the outer-diameter of the brass case to match the size of the visable base of the round filament bulb.

30cm(h) x 15cm(w) x 15cm(d)

Spire

Copper, 3D printed plastic, filament bulb, fabric cord. 2017

I’ve always been facinated with the warmth of a copper pipe and how it’s tone feels at one with the glow of a filament bulb. I choose matt black plastic to act as a visual grounding device, keeping the bare metal in-check.

Adjustable light height, sliding
 Adjustable light direction, rotating

80cm(h) x 30cm(w) x 30cm(d)
