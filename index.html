Tower

Copper, 3D printed plastic, LED bulb, fabric cord. 2023

Further exploration into the warmth of copper. This evolution of an earlier light is enhanced to provide maximum customisation of lighting utility with two independently configurable spotlights.

Adjustable, 90° vertical & 360° horizontal

200cm(h) x 50cm(w) x 50cm(d)

Candle

3D printed plastic, Acrylic rod, LED diode, rechargable battery. 2022

With a looming energy crisis this winter and the many thoughts of buying fail-safe backup candles, I wanted to explore what a modern digital candle might be like. Safe, portable, rechargable, bright enough to navigate at night.

Press the top of the Acrylic rod to turn on and off

20cm(h) x 6cm(w) x 4cm(d)

Rust

Found steel plates, 3D printed plastic, fabric cord. 2021

Discovered during a hike at Blue Lake near Telluride, Colorado, these rusted plates feature consistent cut corners and years worth of unique weathered patterns. By placing the cut corners in pairs the negative space created provides a functional notch for the cord (rear-bottom) and an aesthetic notch which reveals the golden glow from inside (front-top).

Created in small & large sizes

S:20/L:30cm(h) x 5cm(w) x 5cm(d)

Frame

Brass, Oak wood, 3D printed plastic, LED bulb, fabric cord. 2021

I designed this light with contrast in mind, two materials – wood and brass – and two curves – top and bottom. The half mirror bulb is hung at eye level, to further emphasis the contrast. My favorite detail is the cord, hidden in a channel in the back of one of the legs.

Designed for corner locations

200cm(h) x 45cm(w) x 5cm(d)

Stem

Brass, 3D printed plastic, filament bulb, fabric cord. 2018

A quick experiment with my brother’s metal lathe and my 3D printer. This slimness of the custom-made E27 socket allowed for the outer-diameter of the brass case to match the size of the visable base of the round filament bulb.

30cm(h) x 15cm(w) x 15cm(d)

Spire

Copper, 3D printed plastic, filament bulb, fabric cord. 2017

I’ve always been fascinated with the warmth of a copper pipe and how it’s tone feels at one with the glow of a filament bulb. I choose matt black plastic to act as a visual grounding device, keeping the bare metal in-check.

Adjustable, vertial sliding & 360° rotating

80cm(h) x 30cm(w) x 30cm(d)
